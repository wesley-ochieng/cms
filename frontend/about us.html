Who We Are

Eden Event Planners is a full service event design and planning company based in Nairobi, Kenya. Eden Event Planners was formed with the goal of designing, producing and managing memorable events as we ensure maximum client satisfaction.In its years of operation, Eden Event Planners has built an impressive events repertoire and clientele without compromising its principles and quality.

Why choose us

At Eden Event Planners, we believe every client is unique, thus every event and concept is original and tailor made to that event.Thanks to our professional team and extensive networks, Eden Event Planners manages to deliver cost effective events to our clients for the best services . We pride ourselves in attention to detail, an amazing customer experience and quality event presentation.
